FexChat App Details
===================

1. UI Theme & Color Palette
---------------------------
The app uses a "Futuristic / Neon" dark theme.

*   **Background Color**: #050510 (Very dark blue/black)
*   **Surface Color**: #101020 (Slightly lighter dark blue)
*   **Primary Color**: #00F0FF (Cyan Neon) - Used for buttons, active elements.
*   **Secondary Color**: #7000FF (Purple Neon) - Used for gradients/accents.
*   **Accent Color**: #FFFF0055 (Pink Neon) - Used for errors or highlights.
*   **Text Colors**: 
    *   Primary: White (#FFFFFF)
    *   Secondary: White with 54% opacity.
*   **Typography**: "Poppins" font family from Google Fonts.

2. App Features
---------------
FexChat is a social networking app focused on real-time communication and daily stories.

*   **Authentication & Security**:
    *   Email/Password Login & Signup.
    *   **Face Verification**: Identity verification using ML Kit face detection. Users must align their face to capture a verification image.

*   **Stories (FexStories)**:
    *   **Daily Limit**: Users can post only ONE story per day.
    *   **Live Capture**: Stories are captured directly from the camera ("Live" feel).
    *   **Spotify Integration**: Users can attach a Spotify track link to their story, which plays via an embedded player.
    *   **Ephemeral**: Stories expire after 24 hours.
    *   **Captions**: Add text captions to stories.

*   **Chat (Real-time Messaging)**:
    *   **Instant Messaging**: Real-time chat powered by Supabase.
    *   **Offline Support**: Messages are cached locally (using SQLite/sqflite) for offline viewing.
    *   **Optimistic UI**: Messages appear instantly before syncing to the server.
    *   **Privacy**: Option to clear chat history (deletes local copy).

*   **Social**:
    *   **User Search**: Search for other users by username.
    *   **Profiles**: View user profiles (username, photo, email).

3. Step-by-Step Usage Guide
---------------------------

### Phase 1: Onboarding
1.  **Sign Up**: Open the app and click "Sign Up". Enter your email and create a password.
2.  **Login**: Use your credentials to log in.
3.  **Face Verification**: 
    *   You will be prompted to verify your identity.
    *   Allow camera permissions.
    *   Align your face within the frame.
    *   Wait for the "Face Detected" and "Verification Successful" messages.

### Phase 2: Connecting with Others
1.  **Find Friends**:
    *   Go to the **Search** tab (Magnifying glass icon).
    *   Type a username in the search bar.
    *   Tap on a user from the results to open a chat.
2.  **Chatting**:
    *   Type your message in the text field and hit send.
    *   Your message sends instantly.
    *   To clear history: Tap the options (if available) or use the specific "Clear Chat" feature (implied in code logic).

### Phase 3: Sharing Your Day (Stories)
1.  **Create a Story**:
    *   Navigate to the **Story** creation screen (usually a Camera icon or "Add Story" button).
    *   **Camera**: The camera opens immediately. Snap a photo of your current moment.
2.  **Customize**:
    *   **Spotify**: Paste a Spotify track link (e.g., `open.spotify.com/track/...`) in the music field.
    *   **Caption**: Write a short description.
3.  **Post**:
    *   Tap **POST TO STORY**.
    *   *Note*: If you have already posted today, the app will prevent a second post.

### Phase 4: Profile & Settings
1.  **Profile**: Go to the Profile tab to view your details.
2.  **Edit**: Use the "Edit Profile" button to update your photo or display name.
